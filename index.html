About Me...


I'm a biomedical researcher by day—semi-competent rock climber, dog lover, craft beer enthusiast, aspiring home brewer, music fan, amateur musician, and wannabe data scientist by night. Ok...I often do a lot of that other stuff during the day too. I work as a post-doctoral fellow at the Institute for Systems Biology in Seattle, WA. I share an apartment with my girlfriend and our two lovely dogs, and I do my best to make the most of what life in Seattle (and the greater Washington state) has to offer.

While I hope that this page might eventually serve as a forum to share my work, my experiences, and perhaps even my thoughts, for now it's mostly just a portal to the various components of my "web presence." As I get a better handle on working with Bootstrap (and actually find the time to build/maintain a website), I'll add some more interesting content.
